Welcome To Crowned K9s Boarding

Personalized boarding in Raleigh, NC. Cozy rooms, structured play, and daily enrichment on our peaceful 1-acre property.

Our Environment

At our home-away-from-home location in Raleigh, NC, we specialize in giving every dog a truly personalized boarding experience. As a family-owned and operated business, we believe your pet deserves the same comfort, safety, and attention they get at home—complete with their own cozy room and plenty of supervised playtime. Our 1-acre countryside property offers a peaceful setting where your pup can relax, explore, and enjoy daily enrichment.

More Than Boarding

Beyond boarding, we also provide day classes and specialized board & train programs to meet each dog’s unique needs. With limited spots available, every guest benefits from one-on-one interaction and a warm, family-like environment that sets our facility apart. We’re here to ensure your furry companion feels right at home.
