Training Programs

Options for every budget: Private Session, Transformation Package, and Academy.

Choose what fits your family: start with a Single Private Session, get our most affordable 3–4 week Transformation Training Package for focused weekly coaching, or maximize results with our Crowned K9s Academy board & train that includes daily progress videos and lifetime support.

Puppy Rehoming Program

Training-first rehoming for the right family fit.

We partner with rescues and breeders who need help placing puppies. We assume responsibility, put each puppy through our Crowned K9s Academy, and rehome them to a loving, prepared family.

Group Classes

Small‑group learning, confidence, and real‑world practice.

Fun, focused classes that build obedience, leash manners, and calm around distractions. Ideal for families who want coaching and socialization with 4–6 dogs per class.

Includes clear homework and support between classes so progress continues at home.
